Answer Writing

How to Study Paper 2 for High Marks

  1. Understand the Hadith theme or historical topic before memorising details.
  2. Learn key terms such as isnad, matn, musnad, musannaf and compilers.
  3. Practise 10-mark answers with accurate detail and logical sequence.
  4. Practise 4-mark answers with reflection, application and significance.
  5. Use topic-wise past papers to recognise repeated question patterns.
