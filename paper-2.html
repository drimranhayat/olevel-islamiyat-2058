Paper 2 Course Plan

Paper 2 preparation is designed to help students master Hadith, Caliphs, beliefs and practices while improving answer structure and evaluation.

Hadith

Major Teachings in Hadith

Students study selected Hadith passages with focus on individual conduct, community life, belief, moral values and practical Islamic guidance.

  • Meaning and message of Hadiths
  • Relevance to Muslim life
  • Individual and social conduct
  • Clear explanation for part (b)
Hadith

History & Importance of Hadith

Students learn Hadith compilation, early collections, isnad and matn, musnad and musannaf collections, and the importance of Hadith in Islamic law.

  • Compilation and preservation
  • Isnad and matn
  • Major compilers and collections
  • Hadith as a source of guidance
Caliphs

Rightly Guided Caliphs

Students study the four Caliphs through events, policies, achievements, difficulties, leadership qualities and their importance for Muslim history.

  • Hazrat Abu Bakr (RA)
  • Hazrat Umar (RA)
  • Hazrat Uthman (RA)
  • Hazrat Ali (RA)

Who Should Join Paper 2 Classes?

Paper 2 classes are helpful for students who need structured support in Hadith, Caliphs, beliefs, practices and evaluation-style answers.

  • Students who confuse Hadith compilation details
  • Students who need clear notes on the Caliphs
  • Students who find Articles of Faith difficult
  • Students who need stronger 4-mark answers
  • Students preparing for school tests, mocks or final exams

Why Paper 2 Needs Special Attention

Paper 2 requires students to combine accurate Islamic knowledge with explanation of significance. Students must describe facts clearly and show why teachings, beliefs and practices matter.

Balanced Preparation

This course balances factual detail, Islamic terminology, religious meaning and exam-focused answer development so students can write with clarity and confidence.

Main Topics Covered in Paper 2

Each area is taught with background knowledge, key terms, repeated question awareness, model answer guidance and past paper practice.

Hadith

Hadith Teachings

Students learn how Hadiths guide personal character, family life, community relations, worship, honesty, charity and responsibility.

Hadith History

Compilation and Authentication

Students study how Hadiths were collected, checked and preserved, including the role of isnad, matn and major collections.

Caliphs

Leadership and Challenges

Students learn the main events, policies and challenges of the Rightly Guided Caliphs and their model of leadership.

Beliefs

Six Articles of Faith

Beliefs are explained through meaning, importance and impact on Muslim thought, worship and conduct.

Practices

Five Pillars of Islam

Students study Shahada, Salat, Zakat, Sawm and Hajj with their method, meaning and practical importance.

Jihad

Wider Meanings of Jihad

Jihad is explained carefully in its spiritual, moral, mental and physical meanings with balanced exam-appropriate wording.

Paper 2 Answer-Writing Training

Students are guided to write answers that are relevant, organised and supported with accurate Islamic knowledge.

Step 1

Recall Relevant Knowledge

Students learn how to choose the correct facts, events, names, terms and examples according to the question.

Step 2

Develop the Answer

Students practise structured paragraphs with explanation, sequence and topic relevance.

Step 3

Explain Significance

Students learn how to write thoughtful part (b) answers about importance and relevance in Muslim life.
