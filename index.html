Syllabus-Aligned Preparation Plan

The course follows the Cambridge O Level Islamiyat 2058 structure: two written papers, both 1½ hours, each carrying 50 marks. Students are trained for knowledge, understanding, and exam-style answer development.

Paper 1

Qur’an, Prophet ﷺ & Early Community

Paper 1 focuses on major Qur’anic themes, history and importance of the Qur’an, the life and importance of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and the first Islamic community.

  • Major themes of the Qur’an
  • History and importance of the Qur’an
  • Life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
  • First Islamic community
Paper 2

Hadith, Caliphs, Beliefs & Practices

Paper 2 focuses on major Hadith teachings, history and importance of Hadith, Rightly Guided Caliphs, Articles of Faith, and Pillars of Islam.

  • Major teachings in Hadith
  • History and importance of Hadith
  • Rightly Guided Caliphs
  • Articles of Faith and Pillars
Assessment Skills

AO1 + AO2 Training

Students are trained to recall and present relevant facts clearly, and to explain the significance of Islamic teachings in belief, worship, character, and Muslim life.

  • AO1: Knowledge and relevant facts
  • AO2: Understanding and significance
  • 10-mark structured answers
  • 4-mark reasoning and evaluation

Paper 1 Preparation

Paper 1 preparation is organized around Qur’anic themes, the Qur’an’s history and importance, the life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and the first Islamic community.

Qur’an

Major Themes of the Qur’an

Students study the selected Qur’anic passages with focus on themes, meaning, Arabic reference, translation, and relevance for Muslims today.

  • Allah in Himself
  • Allah’s relationship with the created world
  • Allah’s Messengers
Qur’an

History & Importance of the Qur’an

Includes revelation from 610 to 632, compilation under the Rightly Guided Caliphs, legal thinking, and the Qur’an’s role as the basis of Islamic thought and action.

Seerah

Life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

Students study Makkan and Madinan life, his leadership, conflicts, actions, character, and his significance as the Seal of the Prophets.

Community

The First Islamic Community

Includes the Prophet’s wives, descendants, leading Companions, Emigrants, Helpers, scribes, and important figures around the Prophet ﷺ.

Writing

10-Mark Answer Writing

Students learn how to write clear, detailed, well-developed answers using accurate facts, Islamic terms, quotations, and relevant examples.

Evaluation

4-Mark Part (b) Answers

Students practise significance-based questions using reasoning, personal response, examples from Muslim life, and clear explanation.

Paper 2 Preparation

Paper 2 preparation covers Hadith passages, Hadith compilation, Rightly Guided Caliphs, Articles of Faith, Pillars of Islam, and Jihad in its wider meanings.

Hadith

Major Teachings in Hadith

Students study selected Hadiths with focus on individual conduct, community life, belief, moral action, and contemporary relevance.

Hadith

History & Importance of Hadith

Includes compilation, early collections, musnad and musannaf collections, isnad and matn, compilers, and Hadith in legal thinking.

Caliphs

Rightly Guided Caliphs

Students study the main events, policies, achievements, difficulties, leadership examples, and relevance of their rule for later Muslim communities.

Beliefs

Articles of Faith

Includes belief in Allah, angels, books, prophets, predestination and decree, resurrection, and the Last Day.

Practices

Pillars of Islam

Includes Shahada, Salat, Zakat, Sawm, and Hajj with their method, meaning, importance, and impact on Muslim life.

Jihad

Jihad: Physical, Mental & Spiritual

Students study Jihad carefully in its range of meanings with balanced explanation, religious context, and exam-appropriate wording.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from students and parents about O Level Islamiyat 2058 and IGCSE Islamiyat 0493 online preparation.

Are the classes online?

Yes. The classes are online, so students can join from Lahore, other cities of Pakistan, or abroad.

Which syllabus is covered?

The course focuses on Cambridge O Level Islamiyat 2058 and also supports IGCSE Islamiyat 0493 students.

Are Paper 1 and Paper 2 both included?

Yes. Paper 1 includes Qur’anic passages, Qur’an history, Seerah, and the first Islamic community. Paper 2 includes Hadith, Caliphs, Articles of Faith, and Pillars of Islam.

Will students practise past papers?

Yes. Past paper practice is included so students learn question patterns, answer structure, timing, and mark scheme expectations.

Will students get notes and worksheets?

Yes. Students receive topic-wise guidance, revision support, worksheets, and model-answer practice according to their preparation needs.

Will answers be checked?

Yes. Students are guided on 10-mark knowledge answers and 4-mark evaluation answers, with correction and feedback where required.

Is there a free trial class?

Yes. Students and parents can book a free online trial class through WhatsApp before joining the regular course.
