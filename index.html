Professional Profile

Dr. Imran Hayat is an accomplished Islamic scholar, educator, and examiner with extensive experience in Cambridge and higher education. He holds a PhD in Islamic Studies and currently serves as a Lecturer in Islamic Studies at the University of Education.

For O Level and IGCSE students, Dr. Hayat is recognized for his experience teaching Cambridge Islamiyat and Arabic at Aitchison College, where he taught Senior School O Level classes from 2014 to 2017.

He was also appointed by the British Council Pakistan as an official IGCSE Arabic Oral Examiner, giving him firsthand insight into international assessment standards and examination requirements.

His approach emphasizes conceptual understanding, effective exam technique, and the application of Islamic teachings to contemporary issues.

Syllabus-Aligned Preparation Plan

The course follows the Cambridge O Level Islamiyat 2058 structure: two written papers, both 1½ hours, each carrying 50 marks. Students are trained for knowledge, understanding, and exam-style answer development.

Past Paper Practice

Paper 1 Preparation

Paper 1 preparation is organized around Qur’anic themes, the Qur’an’s history and importance, the life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and the first Islamic community.

Qur’an

Major Themes of the Qur’an

Students study selected Qur’anic passages with focus on themes, meaning, Arabic reference, translation, and relevance for Muslims today.

Qur’an

History & Importance of the Qur’an

Includes revelation from 610 to 632, compilation under the Rightly Guided Caliphs, legal thinking, and the Qur’an’s role as the basis of Islamic thought and action.

Seerah

Life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

Students study Makkan and Madinan life, his leadership, conflicts, actions, character, and his significance as the Seal of the Prophets.

Paper 2 Preparation

Paper 2 preparation covers Hadith passages, Hadith compilation, Rightly Guided Caliphs, Articles of Faith, Pillars of Islam, and Jihad in its wider meanings.

Hadith

Major Teachings in Hadith

Students study selected Hadiths with focus on individual conduct, community life, belief, moral action, and contemporary relevance.

Caliphs

Rightly Guided Caliphs

Students study the main events, policies, achievements, difficulties, leadership examples, and relevance of their rule for later Muslim communities.

Beliefs & Practices

Articles of Faith & Pillars

Includes belief in Allah, angels, books, prophets, predestination, resurrection, and the Five Pillars of Islam.

Teaching Method

The course follows a practical process that moves students from understanding topics to writing exam-ready answers.

1

2

3

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from students and parents about O Level Islamiyat 2058 and IGCSE Islamiyat 0493 online preparation.

Are the classes online?

Yes. The classes are online, so students can join from Lahore, other cities of Pakistan, or abroad.

Which syllabus is covered?

The course focuses on Cambridge O Level Islamiyat 2058 and also supports IGCSE Islamiyat 0493 students.

Are Paper 1 and Paper 2 both included?

Yes. Paper 1 includes Qur’anic passages, Qur’an history, Seerah, and the first Islamic community. Paper 2 includes Hadith, Caliphs, Articles of Faith, and Pillars of Islam.

Will students practise past papers?

Yes. Past paper practice is included so students learn question patterns, answer structure, timing, and mark scheme expectations.
