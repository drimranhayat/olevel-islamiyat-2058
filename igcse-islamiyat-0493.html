Why IGCSE Students Need Guided Preparation

Many students understand Islamiyat topics generally, but they need practice in selecting relevant details, explaining significance, and writing answers in a focused exam style.

Understanding + Writing

The course combines topic explanation with writing practice. Students are guided to move from memorised notes towards clear, structured and relevant answers.

Main Areas Covered in IGCSE Islamiyat 0493

The course gives attention to both knowledge and understanding, helping students connect Islamic teachings with belief, worship, conduct, and community life.

Qur’an

Qur’anic Themes

Students learn the central themes of selected Qur’anic passages and how these themes guide Muslim belief and action.

Qur’an

History of the Qur’an

Students study revelation, preservation, compilation, and the Qur’an’s importance as the central source of Islamic guidance.

Seerah

Life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

Key events from Makkan and Madinan life are taught with their importance for Muslims and relevance to Islamic history.

Hadith

Hadith Teachings

Hadith passages are explained with focus on individual conduct, community life, moral values, and practical guidance.

Caliphs

Rightly Guided Caliphs

Students study the achievements, challenges, policies, and leadership examples of the Rightly Guided Caliphs.

Faith & Practice

Beliefs and Practices

Students learn the importance of Articles of Faith, Pillars of Islam, and Islamic values in shaping Muslim life.
