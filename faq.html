General Questions

These FAQs help students and parents understand how the online Islamiyat preparation works.

Are the classes online?

Yes. Classes are online, so students can join from Lahore, other cities of Pakistan or abroad.

Which syllabus is covered?

The course focuses on Cambridge O Level Islamiyat 2058 and also supports IGCSE Islamiyat 0493 students.

Is there a free trial class?

Yes. Students and parents can book a free online trial class before joining the regular course.

Are Paper 1 and Paper 2 both included?

Yes. Full support is available for both Paper 1 and Paper 2, including topic explanation, revision and past paper practice.

Course and Exam Preparation FAQs

These questions focus on syllabus coverage, answer writing and exam preparation.

What is covered in Paper 1?

Paper 1 includes Qur’anic passages and themes, history and importance of the Qur’an, the life and importance of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and the first Islamic community.

What is covered in Paper 2?

Paper 2 includes Hadith teachings, history and importance of Hadith, Rightly Guided Caliphs, Articles of Faith, Pillars of Islam and Jihad.

Will students practise past papers?

Yes. Past paper practice is included to help students understand question patterns, answer structure, timing and mark expectations.

Will answers be checked?

Students are guided on 10-mark knowledge answers and 4-mark evaluation answers. Answer correction and feedback can be included according to the course plan.

Will students receive notes and worksheets?

Yes. Students receive topic-wise guidance, revision support, worksheets and model-answer practice according to their preparation needs.

Is the course suitable for weak students?

Yes. The course can begin from basic concepts and gradually build towards exam-ready answers.
