Professional Introduction

Dr. Imran Hayat is an accomplished Islamic scholar, educator, and examiner with extensive experience in Cambridge and higher education.

He holds a PhD in Islamic Studies and currently serves as a Lecturer in Islamic Studies at the University of Education. His academic career combines classroom teaching, curriculum development, research, and examiner responsibilities.

For O Level and IGCSE students, Dr. Hayat is particularly recognized for his experience teaching Cambridge Islamiyat and Arabic at Aitchison College, where he taught Senior School O Level classes from 2014 to 2017.

He was also appointed by the British Council Pakistan as an official IGCSE Arabic Oral Examiner, giving him firsthand insight into international assessment standards and examination requirements.
