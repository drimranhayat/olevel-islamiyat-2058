Professional Introduction

Dr. Imran Hayat is an Islamic scholar and educator with experience in Cambridge-stream teaching and higher education. His academic profile gives parents and students confidence that Islamiyat is being taught with knowledge, seriousness and proper academic discipline.

He holds a PhD in Islamic Studies and currently serves as a Lecturer in Islamic Studies at the University of Education. His work combines teaching, academic instruction, research awareness and curriculum-focused guidance.

For O Level and IGCSE students, his profile is especially relevant because he has taught Cambridge Islamiyat and Arabic at Aitchison College Senior School O Level classes from 2014 to 2017.

He was also appointed by the British Council Pakistan as an official IGCSE Arabic Oral Examiner, giving him insight into international assessment standards and examination discipline.

Teaching Method for O Level Islamiyat 2058

Islamiyat preparation should not be limited to memorising notes. Students need understanding, structure, relevance, confidence and exam practice.

Clarity

Conceptual Understanding

Topics are explained with background, meaning, significance and exam relevance so students understand what they are writing.

Practice

Regular Answer Training

Students practise structured responses for 10-mark and 4-mark questions with attention to relevance, detail, explanation and conclusion.

Application

Islamic Teachings Today

Students are encouraged to understand how Islamic teachings apply to belief, worship, character, community life and contemporary situations.
