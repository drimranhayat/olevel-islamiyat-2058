How to Use This Page

Do not memorise random paragraphs. Learn every passage through theme, key Qur’anic idea, importance for Muslims today, and exam-style bullet points.

1. Identify the Theme

Ask: What does this passage teach about Allah, creation, guidance, judgement, prophethood or worship?

2. Use Passage Words

Refer to important words and ideas from the passage, but do not write unsupported general statements.

3. Explain Importance

Show how the theme affects Muslim belief, worship, conduct, gratitude, accountability and daily life.

4. Practise Timing

For each selected passage, prepare a focused answer for part (a) and a separate answer for part (b).

Question 1 Answer Method

Use this simple structure for every Qur’anic passage. It keeps answers relevant, clear and mark-scheme friendly.

For Part (a): Main Theme

What to include

  • Name the main theme clearly.
  • Use words and ideas from the passage.
  • Explain what the passage teaches about Allah, creation, guidance, judgement or messengers.
  • Write developed bullet points, not vague one-line comments.

What to avoid

  • Do not write the whole translation as your answer.
  • Do not mix the wrong theme group with the passage.
  • Do not write general Islamic knowledge without linking it to the printed passage.
  • Do not ignore key words such as Creator, Lord, mercy, guidance, knowledge and worship.

For Part (b): Importance of Theme

What to include

  • Explain how the theme affects Muslims today.
  • Link the theme with belief, worship, morality, gratitude, responsibility or trust in Allah.
  • Give practical examples such as prayer, avoiding shirk, seeking knowledge, repentance, charity or patience.
  • Use reasoned explanation, not just description.

High-grade sentence starters

  • This teaches Muslims that...
  • In daily life, this matters because...
  • This strengthens a Muslim’s faith by...
  • This reminds Muslims to...

Allah & the Created World

  • How does Sura 99 teach accountability?

Allah’s Messengers

  • Explain the theme of guidance in Sura 6:75–79.
