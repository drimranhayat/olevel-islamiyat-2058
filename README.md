# O Level Islamiyat (2058)

A comprehensive collection of teaching and learning resources for **Cambridge O Level Islamiyat (2058)**, developed by **Dr. Imran Hayat, PhD**.

This repository is designed to support students, teachers, and institutions preparing for Cambridge Islamiyat examinations through structured notes, model answers, lesson plans, worksheets, and exam-focused materials.

## About the Author

**Dr. Imran Hayat** is an Islamic scholar, educator, and researcher with extensive experience in Cambridge education. He has taught O Level Islamiyat and Arabic at leading institutions, including **Aitchison College**, and has served as a **British Council IGCSE Examiner**. His academic work combines rigorous scholarship with practical classroom experience to help students achieve excellence in examinations and develop a deeper understanding of Islam.

## Learning Objectives

By using these resources, students will be able to:

* Understand key Islamic beliefs and practices.
* Analyze Quranic passages and Hadith teachings.
* Apply Islamic teachings to contemporary issues.
* Develop effective examination techniques.
* Produce high-quality Cambridge-style answers.
