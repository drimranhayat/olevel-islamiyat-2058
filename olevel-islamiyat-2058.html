O Level Islamiyat 2058 Course Plan

This course is designed for students who want structured preparation, clear explanation, and strong answer-writing practice for Cambridge O Level Islamiyat 2058.

Paper 1

Qur’an, Prophet ﷺ & Early Community

Paper 1 preparation focuses on Qur’anic themes, history and importance of the Qur’an, the life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and the first Islamic community.

  • Major themes of the Qur’an
  • Revelation and compilation
  • Makkan and Madinan life
  • Wives, descendants, Companions, Emigrants and Helpers
Paper 2

Hadith, Caliphs, Beliefs & Practices

Paper 2 preparation focuses on Hadith teachings, Hadith compilation, Rightly Guided Caliphs, Articles of Faith, Pillars of Islam, and Jihad.

  • Major teachings in Hadith
  • History and importance of Hadith
  • Rightly Guided Caliphs
  • Articles of Faith and Pillars of Islam
Exam Skills

Answer Writing & Evaluation

Students are trained to write detailed, relevant, and well-organised answers that meet the demands of knowledge and understanding questions.

  • 10-mark knowledge answer structure
  • 4-mark evaluation answer method
  • Use of relevant Islamic terms
  • Past paper timing and planning

Why Focus on 2058 Separately?

O Level Islamiyat 2058 has a clear exam structure. Students need to understand not only the topics but also how to answer according to exam expectations.

Content + Technique

Many students memorise notes but lose marks because their answers lack structure, relevance, explanation, or evaluation. This course combines content mastery with answer-writing technique.

Main Preparation Areas

Each part of the course is taught with background explanation, key facts, important terms, repeated question awareness, and answer practice.

Qur’an

Major Themes of the Qur’an

Students study selected Qur’anic passages with attention to theme, meaning, message, and relevance for Muslims today.

Seerah

Life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

Students learn key events from Makkan and Madinan life, including leadership, migration, community-building, and character.

Hadith

Hadith Teachings

Hadith passages are taught with explanation of conduct, belief, community life, and practical application.

Caliphs

Rightly Guided Caliphs

Students study main events, policies, achievements, difficulties, and leadership lessons from the four Caliphs.

Beliefs

Articles of Faith

Beliefs are explained with meaning, importance, and impact on Muslim thought, worship, and daily life.

Practices

Pillars of Islam

Students learn the method, meaning, importance, and personal/social impact of the Five Pillars.

Answer-Writing Training

The course gives special attention to how students should write in exams, because strong content alone is not enough without proper organisation and explanation.

Step 1

Build Accurate Knowledge

Students first learn the relevant facts, names, events, terms, and quotations needed for each topic.

Step 2

Organise the Answer

Students practise paragraph structure, sequencing of points, and keeping the answer focused on the question.

Step 3

Explain Significance

Students learn how to explain importance, relevance, and application of Islamic teachings in Muslim life.
