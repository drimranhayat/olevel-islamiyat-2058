What Is O Level Islamiyat 2058?

Cambridge O Level Islamiyat 2058 is a syllabus-based examination course that helps students study the major sources of Islam, its main beliefs, early Islamic history, and the importance of Islamic teachings in Muslim life.

Paper 1

Qur’an, Seerah and First Islamic Community

Paper 1 focuses on Qur’anic themes, the history and importance of the Qur’an, the life and importance of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and the first Islamic community.

  • Major themes of the Qur’an
  • History and importance of the Qur’an
  • Life and importance of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
  • The first Islamic community

Exam Structure for O Level Islamiyat 2058

The course is arranged around the actual examination pattern so that students understand both the syllabus content and the way the exam is attempted.

Paper 1

Paper 1 Structure

Paper 1 contains five questions. Students must answer Question 1, Question 2, and two other questions.

  • Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Total marks: 50
  • Major themes of the Qur’an
  • History and importance of the Qur’an
  • Life and importance of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ
  • First Islamic community
Paper 2

Paper 2 Structure

Paper 2 also contains five questions. Students must answer Question 1, Question 2, and two other questions.

  • Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Total marks: 50
  • Major teachings in the Hadiths
  • History and importance of the Hadiths
  • Rightly Guided Caliphs
  • Articles of Faith and Pillars of Islam

Important Study Message

Students should not treat Islamiyat as memorisation only. Strong answers need accurate knowledge, clear selection of relevant facts, paragraph development, and explanation of significance in Islamic belief and Muslim life.

Complete Topic Coverage

The preparation is arranged in a clean, topic-wise sequence so students and parents can easily track syllabus progress.

Paper 1 Coverage

Paper 1 develops understanding of the Qur’an, the Prophet’s ﷺ life, and the people who formed the first Islamic community.

  • God in Himself
  • God’s relationship with the created world
  • God’s Messengers
  • Revelation of the Qur’an between 610 and 632
  • Compilation of the Qur’an under the Rightly Guided Caliphs
  • Qur’an in legal thinking and its relationship with Hadith, ijma‘ and qiyas
  • Main events of the Prophet’s ﷺ Makkan and Madinan life
  • The Prophet’s ﷺ actions, character and significance as Seal of the Prophets
  • Wives, descendants, Companions, scribes, Muhajirun and Ansar

Paper 2 Coverage

Paper 2 develops understanding of Hadith, leadership, Islamic beliefs, duties, practices, and their significance.

  • Major teachings in the Hadiths: individual conduct and community life
  • History of Hadith compilation
  • Earliest collections, musnad and musannaf collections
  • Isnad and matn as methods of testing reliability
  • Six Sunni collections and four Shi‘a collections
  • Rules of Abu Bakr, Umar, Uthman and Ali رضي الله عنهم
  • Six Articles of Faith and Jihad in its range of meanings
  • Five Pillars of Islam: Shahada, Salat, Zakat, Sawm and Hajj

Answer-Writing Training
