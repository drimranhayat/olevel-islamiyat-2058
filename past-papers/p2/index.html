How to Use These Past Paper Pages

  1. Open one topic and read the repeated question pattern carefully.
  2. Identify whether the question demands description, explanation, comparison or significance.
  3. Prepare a clear 10-mark answer with accurate facts, sequence and development.
  4. Practise the 4-mark part with reasoning, relevance and Islamic teaching.
  5. Rewrite weak answers into stronger answers using examiner-style improvement.

A* Answer Focus

  • Accurate dates, names and terms where relevant
  • Clear link with the wording of the question
  • Developed explanation, not memorised storytelling only
  • Relevant Qur’an/Hadith reference where suitable
  • Balanced and thoughtful 4-mark evaluation
