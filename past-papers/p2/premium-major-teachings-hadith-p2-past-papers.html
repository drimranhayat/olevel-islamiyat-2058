High-scoring preparation guide

Exam move
For 10-mark answers, use at least four relevant set Hadiths where the question asks for them; do not merely quote—explain the teaching and its practical impact.
Exam move
For 4-mark answers, give two developed reasons with a clear contemporary application: individual reform, family life, mosque/community, social justice, or unity.
Exam move
Avoid vague moral statements. Link each point to a Hadith teaching such as sincerity, brotherhood, removing harm, mercy, lawful conduct, or obedience to the Prophet ﷺ.
