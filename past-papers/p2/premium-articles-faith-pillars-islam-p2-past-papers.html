High-scoring preparation guide

Exam move
For Articles of Faith, combine belief, Qur’anic concept, examples, and practical effect on daily life.
Exam move
For Pillars, always cover both method and significance: how it is performed and why it matters spiritually, morally and socially.
Exam move
Jihad questions require range and balance: self, tongue, knowledge, wealth/service and armed struggle with strict rules and limits.
