High-scoring preparation guide

Exam move
Learn the vocabulary precisely: isnad, matn, musnad, musannaf, sahih, hasan, da‘if, mawdu‘, Hadith Qudsi and Hadith Nabawi.
Exam move
For authenticity questions, split the answer into narrator checks and text checks; include reliability, memory, character, continuity, agreement with Qur’an and established Sunnah.
Exam move
For Islamic law questions, show hierarchy and relationship: Qur’an as primary source, Hadith/Sunnah as explanation and practice, then ijma‘ and qiyas for new issues.
