Exam-Focused Revision Strategy

For best results, students should not only read the questions. They should identify repeated themes, prepare answer plans, memorise key facts, and practise 10-mark and 4-mark answers separately.

1. Track Repeated Questions

Mark questions that appear again and again. These topics usually show strong exam importance.

2. Prepare 10-Mark Content

For part A, focus on detailed knowledge, sequence of events, names, terms, and clear explanation.

3. Practise 4-Mark Evaluation

For part B, write judgement with Islamic reasoning, examples and clear relevance.
