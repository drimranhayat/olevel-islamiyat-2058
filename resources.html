How to Use These Resources

Students should not simply read notes. They should use each resource actively for revision and answer practice.

  1. Open one topic and read it carefully.
  2. Highlight key facts, names, terms and references.
  3. Attempt a related past paper question.
  4. Compare your answer with a model answer structure.
  5. Rewrite the answer after feedback and correction.
