How to Use These Resources

Students should not simply read notes. They should use each resource actively for revision and answer practice.

  • Read one topic carefully
  • Highlight key facts and terms
  • Attempt a past paper question
  • Compare with a model answer structure
  • Rewrite the answer after feedback
