Feedback Policy

This website should only publish genuine parent or student feedback. No fake testimonials, invented reviews, or exaggerated claims should be added.

  • Use real feedback only.
  • Ask permission before publishing names, initials or screenshots.
  • Protect student privacy.
  • Avoid promises such as guaranteed grades.
  • Keep feedback short, honest and relevant.
Note: Real testimonials can be added later when parents or students give permission.

What Parents May Check

Before joining, parents can ask simple and practical questions about the student’s preparation.

  1. Is the student preparing for O Level 2058 or IGCSE 0493?
  2. Does the student need Paper 1, Paper 2 or both?
  3. Is the main weakness content, revision or answer writing?
  4. How much time is left before the exam?
  5. Will the student practise past paper questions?
  6. Can written answers be checked for improvement?
